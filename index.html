ABOUT ME

I am a San Francisco based developer who strives to build beautiful and meaningful web applications. I first started programming late in high-school when I discovered Q-Basic on the large desktop machine that dominated our living room. When I started college, I expanded my knowlege of software development by taking several upper-division computer science courses, including algorithm design and analysis, object oriented programming, and the C++. More recently, I attended App Academy, a twelve week immersive program focused on web technologies and frameworks, including Ruby, Rails, JavaScript, and Backbone.js. I am always looking for new opportunities. Feel free to drop me a line – I'd love to hear from you!
